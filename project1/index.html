Inception

Genre: Sci-Fi | Release Year: 2010

Dreams Within Dreams

Synopsis

Dom Cobb, a skilled thief who extracts secrets from people's dreams, is offered a chance at redemption: instead of stealing information, he must plant an idea into the mind of a powerful businessman. This seemingly impossible task, known as "inception," takes Cobb and his team on a dangerous journey through multiple layers of shared dream worlds, where they face projections of the target's subconscious and struggle with Cobb's own past.
